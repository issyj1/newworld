UNIQLO - NO LOGO

People are buying less into heavily branded clothing and unlike other big brands, Uniqlo’s clothing is all unbranded.

Activation: The Debranding Service

A service where you can take your branded clothing into Uniqlo stores and get the branding removed.

Activation: The clothes drop hijack Uniqlo will hijack clothes drops by offering Uniqlo freebies alongside huge queues, diverting people away from branded clothing and towards Uniqlo clothing.
